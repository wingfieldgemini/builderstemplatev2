Who We Are

Two Decades of Building Excellence

Founded in 2003 by master builder James Harwood, Ironbark Construction started with a simple philosophy: build it right, build it strong, build it once.

What began as a small residential building crew has grown into one of the region's most trusted construction firms, delivering over 350 projects across residential, commercial, and heritage sectors.
