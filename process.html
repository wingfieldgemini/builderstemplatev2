Five Steps to Your New Build

Every Ironbark project follows our proven five-stage process, keeping you informed and in control at every step.

Step 01

Consultation

It starts with a conversation. We meet on-site or at our office to understand your vision, requirements, and budget. We'll assess your site, discuss design ideas, and provide an initial feasibility overview ‚Äî all free of charge.

Free site visit Needs assessment Budget guidance Feasibility review
Step 02

Design & Planning

Working with architects and designers, we refine your concept into detailed plans. We coordinate engineering, energy modelling, and material specifications to ensure your build is optimised for performance and budget.

Architect coordination Detailed plans Material selection Cost planning
Step 03

Consent & Approvals

We handle the paperwork. Our team prepares and lodges all building consent applications, liaises with council, and manages the approval process so you don't have to.

Consent lodgement Council liaison Resource consents Documentation
Step 04

Construction

The exciting part. Our experienced crews bring your plans to life with precision craftsmanship. You'll receive weekly progress updates, and your dedicated project manager is always available to answer questions.

Dedicated PM Weekly updates Quality checks Safety compliance
Step 05

Handover

We walk through your completed project together, addressing any final details. You receive all documentation, warranties, and compliance certificates. Our 12-month defects liability period gives you total peace of mind.

Final walkthrough Documentation pack Warranties 12-month defects cover
